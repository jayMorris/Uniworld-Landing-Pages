2018 Pre-and Post-Cruise/Tour Extensions

While you’re planning your trip of a lifetime, why not extend the excitement? Our carefully curated pre- and post-cruise/tour extensions make it easy to add a few days to the front or back end of your trip, while leaving the planning to us.

Our packages include a variety of hotel options, transfers and a dedicated destination expert to escort you along the way. We’ve designed each package to offer a high level of luxury and service, taking the Uniworld experience from ship to shore. The only thing better than taking a Uniworld journey? Taking a longer one.

ME TO WE

Our new partnership with ME to WE combines authentic volunteering with sightseeing in India, so you can delve into the local culture while helping to make a difference in this fascinating, diverse and complex area of the world. This 5-day pre-cruise extension to our “India’s Golden Triangle & the Sacred Ganges” cruise and tour will place you in the heart of Udaipur to work alongside local residents on a development project.
